The Last Waltz Show Concept

The Last Waltz was a documentary made by the Canadian group called The Band a few years ago showcasing their last concert and they invited on stage many other musicians to play songs with them. It was called The Last Waltz because it was their final concert and it's kind of a concept that means that. Also a waltz is a dance with 3/4 time signature and my concert has mostly 3 genres which are rock, some metal, and bluegrass.

I inspired my upcoming show to have that same name because it's the concert that would showcase the end of my Berklee Journey.

The process for the setlist creation and the artists selected for the show

For my show I wanted my band to be some of my good friends and people that are very knowledgeable in a lot of music genres and just technically.

For the song selections, it's mostly songs that I like and songs that when I listen to them it brings me good memories... It's a mixed bag but it's a lot of songs with mostly rock, metal and bluegrass and just songs that are nice to be played and heard live.
