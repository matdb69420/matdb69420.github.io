How connections through Berklee influenced my artistry and performance experience

Connections through classmates was a very important thing and it really enhanced my experience playing with different people and seeing what their ideas are and how they direct things in the band and just how band dynamics are.

A solid connection through a classmate led Mathis to perform with his band the Bubbleheads in Saint-Louis, and with Josephine Glass in both New York City and Los Angeles.

Mathis performing with Josephine Glass and Max Polsky in New York City. November 2022.

Billy Strings and a bluegrass lab... How I got into bluegrass

Bluegrass is very simple harmonically. It's only 1-4-5 chords, but it's very fast and it's kind of a return to basics. Because it's a lot of open strings and major pentatonic scales and open chords... And it's also a very festive kind of music genre. It's fun to play but it's also very fun to listen to.

One of the set standards is called the Beaumont Rag and de Beaumont happens to be my last name so I decided to learn that one. And that one is tricky. It's a harder song to play but it's very fun.
